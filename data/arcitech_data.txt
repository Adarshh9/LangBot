About Arcitech AI
At Arcitech AI, we are at the forefront of revolutionizing industries through artificial intelligence. As a pivotal arm of Arcot Media Pvt Ltd, Arcitech AI is dedicated to advancing technological innovation and accelerating business growth across a diverse spectrum of industries including legal, educational, entertainment, and event management.

Our Vision
Our vision is to harness the transformative power of AI to create innovative solutions that not only solve complex problems but also enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of business processes. We aim to be the leading provider of AI-driven solutions, known for our commitment to innovation, quality, and reliability.

What We Do
Arcitech AI specializes in integrating AI technologies into practical applications for business. Our solutions leverage Large Language Models (LLMs), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Generative AI, and advanced computer vision techniques to deliver tailored services that meet our clients' unique needs.

Our Expertise Includes:
- AI-Powered Software Development: From custom AI algorithms to complete digital transformations, our software development services ensure businesses stay ahead of the technology curve.
- Educational Tools and Platforms: Utilizing AI to enhance learning experiences, offering personalized educational paths, and optimizing management systems.
- Legal Industry Automation: Implementing AI to streamline complex processes such as legal research, contract management, and compliance automation.
- Event Management Solutions: Employing AI-driven tools to plan and personalize events, improving both the scale and quality of event management services.
- Innovative Design and Production: In the textile and design industries, we apply AI to enhance creativity and efficiency in product development and market analysis.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and deploy robust AI solutions using Python and Django, ensuring high performance and responsiveness to requests from the front-end.
- Build and maintain scalable machine learning models from scratch, including but not limited to NLP, computer vision, and generative models.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand end-user requirements and formulate use cases that can be translated into practical AI-driven applications.
- Integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic, utilizing Django frameworks to create network applications.
- Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability while ensuring application security and data protection.
- Stay current with industry trends and emerging technologies in AI and programming languages to incorporate into the infrastructure.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or a related field.
- Proven experience as a Python Developer with expertise in Django framework.
- Strong experience in AI technologies including LLMs, NLP, Generative AI, and OpenCV.
- Demonstrated experience in machine learning, with a portfolio of models built and deployed from scratch.
- Proficiency with AI and machine learning frameworks (such as TensorFlow, PyTorch) and libraries (such as scikit-learn, NLTK, SpaCy).
- Excellent understanding of server-side templating languages and front-end technologies.
- Solid understanding of the entire web development process (design, development, and deployment).
- Strong unit test and debugging skills.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with cloud services (AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure) and ML model deployment.
- Familiarity with containerization and orchestration technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes).
- Contributions to open-source projects or active participation in the AI technology community.
